About Ingredient Scanner

A cleaner-label ingredient tool for fast shopping decisions.

Ingredient Scanner is designed for ingredient-conscious shoppers who want a stricter read on whether an ingredient panel feels simple, minimally processed, and aligned with a cleaner-label standard.

What The App Does

Turns ingredient questions into readable signals.

The app can take one ingredient or a full label, compare it against a growing policy catalog, and return a signal such as okay, caution, avoid, or unknown.

  • Policy-backed means the ingredient matched a curated record in the app's catalog.
  • Heuristic-only means the ingredient looks real, but does not yet have a curated policy record.
  • Unknown means the app could not confidently identify the ingredient and should not pretend otherwise.

Why It Exists

Fast shopping help

Grocery labels are full of additives, vague flavor terms, engineered emulsifiers, and industrial oils that many people want to understand quickly. The goal is not to replace all nuance, but to make first-pass decisions easier.

How To Use It

Two main workflows

  • Paste one ingredient to get a focused ingredient guide.
  • Paste a full ingredient list to get a product-level verdict.
  • Photo scanning can become the second input method later.

Important Disclaimer

Positioning and limits

Ingredient Scanner reflects a stricter cleaner-label and ingredient-conscious viewpoint. It is educational guidance only. It is not medical advice, not nutrition counseling, not a diagnosis tool, and not an official regulatory or legal determination of safety.

Ingredient Scanner is best used as a decision-support tool for people who already care about cleaner labels and want a faster first pass.
