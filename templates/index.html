Cleaner-Label Ingredient Scanner

This tool is built for ingredient-conscious shoppers who want a stricter, cleaner-label read on what is in a product. It is not medical advice and it does not replace personal judgment, but it can help you spot ingredients that are more processed, vague, or strongly out of step with a simple ingredient standard.
